About Me

Xin Kang is an Associate Professor of Computer Science in the Faculty of Science and Technology, Tokushima University, Japan. He received his PhD in Systems Innovation Engineering from Tokushima University in 2013. His research interests include Affective Computing, Neuro-Symbolic AI, Multimodal Machine Learning, and Knowledge Engineering. He has published over 100 papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences. Dr. Kang serves on the editorial board of the Cognitive Robotics journal and is a member of IEEE, ACM, ACL, and CAAI.

Research Interests

  • Affective Computing: Complex Emotion Recognition, Depression Detection, Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis
  • Neuro-Symbolic AI: Transparent AI, Agentic AI, Fuzzy Logic
  • Multimodal Machine Learning: Video-Speech-Text Alignment, Large Multimodal Models
  • Knowledge Engineering: Semantic Information Extraction, Conceptual Modeling, Object-Process Methodology
