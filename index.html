About Me

Dr. Xin Kang is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science, Faculty of Science and Technology, Tokushima University, Japan. He received his PhD in Systems Innovation Engineering from Tokushima University in 2013. Dr. Kang supervises PhD, Master’s, and 4th-year undergraduate students, guiding research projects in Affective Computing, Neuro-Symbolic AI, Multimodal Machine Learning, and Knowledge Engineering. He has published over 100 papers in prestigious peer-reviewed journals and conferences. Dr. Kang serves on the editorial board of the journal Cognitive Robotics and is a member of IEEE, ACM, ACL, and CAAI.

Research Interests

  • Affective Computing: Complex Emotion Recognition, Depression Detection, Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis
  • Neuro-Symbolic AI: Neuro-Conceptual AI, Transparent AI, Agentic AI
  • Multimodal Machine Learning: Multimodal Alignment, Large Multimodal Models
  • Knowledge Engineering: Semantic Information Extraction, Conceptual Modeling, Object-Process Methodology
